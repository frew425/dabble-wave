Converting Volunteer Time to Relationship Currency

  • 1. Submit Hours

    Once you have volunteered, submit your hours to organization.
  • 2. Approval

    Organizations will approve, deny, or modify hours, then notify you on your dashboard.
  • 3. Save Credits

    Accumulate credits each time you volunteer and save them to use for Rewards.
  • 4. Claim Rewards

    Redeem credits for rewards such as tuition, healthcare, and much more!
