Who is the Volunteer Economy?

The Volunteer Economy is a 501c3 charity created by serial entrepreneur and founder, Paul Shirley, in 2009. The WAVE charity financially sponsors the WAVE Commons, a non-profit member-owned collaborative that gives individuals from all walks of life the opportunity to put their unique gifts to work by connecting them with a broad range of volunteer opportunities.

Free Tuition & Fees for Volunteer Members Who Qualify

Our Mission

Provide timely and affordable access to education, healthcare & economic opportunities for volunteers.

Provide opportunities to serve & be served for the well-being of our environment, health, education, economy & social justice.
